Privacy Policy

Chronion — Last updated: June 2026

No data collection

Chronion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party. The developer has no access to your data at any time.

Local storage

All data you enter — timers, sessions, and categories — is stored locally on your device.

iCloud sync

If you are signed into iCloud on your device, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. The developer cannot access your iCloud data.

No third-party services

Chronion does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
